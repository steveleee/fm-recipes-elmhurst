鱼香茄子 Garlic Eggplant

A flavorful Chinese eggplant dish with a garlic-forward sauce. This DASH-friendly version reduces sodium and oil while maintaining the dish's signature taste.

Servings
4 people
Prep Time
15 mins
Cook Time
15 mins

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Chinese Eggplant 4 medium, cut into strips
  • Bell Peppers 2 medium, sliced
  • Mushrooms 1 cup, sliced

Aromatics

  • Garlic 6 cloves, minced
  • Ginger 2-inch piece, minced
  • Scallions 4, chopped

Sauce

  • Rice Vinegar 2 tablespoons
  • Stock 1/2 cup low-sodium vegetable stock
  • Cornstarch 1 tablespoon mixed with water
  • Sesame Oil 1 teaspoon

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Cut eggplant into strips and soak in water for 10 minutes.

  2. 2.

    Drain and pat dry eggplant thoroughly.

  3. 3.

    Heat a non-stick wok over medium-high heat.

  4. 4.

    Add eggplant and dry-fry until slightly softened.

  5. 5.

    Add garlic, ginger, and half the scallions. Stir-fry until fragrant.

  6. 6.

    Add bell peppers and mushrooms, cook for 3-4 minutes.

  7. 7.

    Add stock, rice vinegar, and bring to a simmer.

  8. 8.

    Stir in cornstarch mixture to thicken sauce.

  9. 9.

    Finish with sesame oil and remaining scallions.

Nutrition Information

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe has been adapted to be DASH-compliant by:

  • Using a dry-frying technique to reduce oil usage
  • Adding bell peppers and mushrooms for extra nutrients
  • Using low-sodium vegetable stock
  • Enhancing flavor with aromatics instead of salt
  • Including additional vegetables for increased potassium content
