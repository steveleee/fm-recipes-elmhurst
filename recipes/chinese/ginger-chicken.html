姜葱鸡 Ginger Chicken

A light and flavorful Cantonese dish featuring tender chicken with a ginger-scallion sauce and Chinese broccoli. This DASH-friendly version reduces sodium while maintaining the dish's aromatic qualities.

Servings
4 people
Prep Time
15 mins
Cook Time
20 mins

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast 1 pound, sliced
  • Chinese Broccoli 2 bunches, trimmed
  • Carrots 2 medium, julienned

Aromatics

  • Ginger 3-inch piece, julienned
  • Scallions 6, cut into 2-inch pieces
  • Garlic 4 cloves, minced

Sauce

  • Chicken Stock 1/2 cup low-sodium
  • Rice Wine 2 tablespoons
  • Sesame Oil 2 teaspoons
  • White Pepper 1/4 teaspoon

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Slice chicken breast against the grain into thin pieces.

  2. 2.

    Blanch Chinese broccoli in boiling water for 2 minutes, then shock in ice water.

  3. 3.

    Heat a wok over high heat. Add 1 teaspoon sesame oil.

  4. 4.

    Add ginger and garlic, stir-fry until fragrant.

  5. 5.

    Add chicken and stir-fry until nearly cooked through.

  6. 6.

    Add carrots and cook for 2 minutes.

  7. 7.

    Add Chinese broccoli, stock, and rice wine. Simmer for 3 minutes.

  8. 8.

    Add scallions, remaining sesame oil, and white pepper. Serve hot.

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe has been adapted to be DASH-compliant by:

  • Using lean chicken breast instead of fattier cuts
  • Adding extra vegetables for increased nutrients and fiber
  • Using low-sodium chicken stock
  • Relying on aromatics (ginger, scallions, garlic) for flavor
  • Minimizing oil while maintaining traditional cooking techniques
