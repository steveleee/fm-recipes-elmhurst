全麦担担面 Whole-Grain Dan Dan Noodles

A healthier take on the classic Sichuan noodle dish, using whole wheat noodles and lean turkey. This DASH-friendly version maintains the signature nutty, spicy flavor while keeping sodium low.

Servings
4 people
Prep Time
15 mins
Cook Time
15 mins

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Whole Wheat Noodles 12 oz
  • Ground Turkey 1 pound, lean (93%)
  • Baby Spinach 4 cups

Sauce

  • Tahini 4 tablespoons
  • Rice Vinegar 2 tablespoons
  • Coconut Aminos 3 tablespoons (replaces soy sauce)
  • Chili Oil 2 teaspoons

Aromatics & Spices

  • Garlic 6 cloves, minced
  • Ginger 2-inch piece, minced
  • Szechuan Pepper 1 teaspoon, ground
  • Scallions 4, chopped

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Cook whole wheat noodles according to package instructions. Reserve 1 cup cooking water.

  2. 2.

    Mix sauce ingredients in a bowl until well combined.

  3. 3.

    Heat a large non-stick wok over medium-high heat.

  4. 4.

    Add ground turkey, breaking it up while cooking until browned.

  5. 5.

    Add garlic, ginger, and half the scallions. Cook until fragrant.

  6. 6.

    Stir in Szechuan pepper and cook for 30 seconds.

  7. 7.

    Add spinach and cook until wilted.

  8. 8.

    Add cooked noodles and sauce mixture, tossing to combine.

  9. 9.

    Add reserved cooking water as needed to achieve desired consistency.

  10. 10.

    Garnish with remaining scallions and serve hot.

Nutrition Information

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe has been specially adapted to be DASH-compliant by:

  • Using whole wheat noodles instead of regular noodles for added fiber
  • Substituting traditional pork with lean ground turkey
  • Replacing soy sauce with coconut aminos to reduce sodium
  • Adding spinach for extra vegetables and nutrients
  • Using tahini for healthy fats and protein
  • Incorporating aromatic spices for flavor without excess sodium
