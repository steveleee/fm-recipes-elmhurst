罗汉斋 Buddha's Delight

A vegetarian Chinese dish packed with various vegetables and plant-based proteins. This DASH-friendly version emphasizes fresh vegetables and reduces sodium while maintaining the traditional flavors.

Servings
6 people
Prep Time
30 mins
Cook Time
15 mins

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • Napa Cabbage 2 cups, chopped
  • Carrots 2 medium, sliced
  • Snow Peas 1 cup
  • Bamboo Shoots 1 cup, sliced
  • Baby Corn 1 cup
  • Water Chestnuts 1 cup, sliced

Proteins

  • Tofu 1 block (400g), cubed
  • Black Mushrooms 8, soaked and sliced

Sauce

  • Vegetable Stock 1 cup low-sodium
  • Ginger 2-inch piece, minced
  • Garlic 3 cloves, minced
  • Sesame Oil 1 tablespoon
  • White Pepper 1/4 teaspoon

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Press tofu for 30 minutes to remove excess water, then cube.

  2. 2.

    Soak dried mushrooms in hot water for 30 minutes, then slice.

  3. 3.

    Heat a large wok over medium-high heat. Add 1 teaspoon sesame oil.

  4. 4.

    Add ginger and garlic, stir-fry until fragrant.

  5. 5.

    Add harder vegetables (carrots, bamboo shoots) first, stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.

  6. 6.

    Add remaining vegetables and mushrooms, cook for 3-4 minutes.

  7. 7.

    Add tofu and vegetable stock, simmer for 5 minutes.

  8. 8.

    Season with white pepper and remaining sesame oil.

Nutrition Information

Calories
220
Protein

Sodium
85mg
Potassium
680mg

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe is naturally DASH-compliant and has been further optimized by:

  • Using a variety of vegetables for increased nutrients and fiber
  • Using low-sodium vegetable stock
  • Minimizing oil while maintaining flavor through aromatics
  • Including plant-based proteins (tofu and mushrooms)
  • Adding water chestnuts and bamboo shoots for texture without added sodium
