姜蒜蒸鸡 Garlic Ginger Steamed Chicken

A light and aromatic steamed chicken dish with fresh ginger and garlic, served with bok choy and brown rice. This DASH-friendly version is low in sodium but rich in flavor.

Servings
4 people
Prep Time
15 mins
Cook Time
15 mins

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Chicken Breast 1 pound, skinless, sliced
  • Bok Choy 2 bunches, trimmed
  • Brown Rice 1 cup, uncooked

Aromatics

  • Ginger 3-inch piece, julienned
  • Garlic 6 cloves, minced
  • Scallions 4, chopped

Sauce

  • Rice Wine 2 tablespoons
  • Sesame Oil 2 teaspoons
  • White Pepper 1/4 teaspoon

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Cook brown rice according to package instructions.

  2. 2.

    Slice chicken breast against the grain into 1/4-inch thick pieces.

  3. 3.

    Marinate chicken with rice wine, half the ginger, and half the garlic for 15 minutes.

  4. 4.

    Arrange chicken on a heat-proof plate that will fit in your steamer.

  5. 5.

    Top with remaining ginger and garlic.

  6. 6.

    Steam chicken for 8-10 minutes until cooked through.

  7. 7.

    In the same steamer, cook bok choy for 2-3 minutes until tender-crisp.

  8. 8.

    Drizzle with sesame oil, sprinkle with white pepper and scallions.

  9. 9.

    Serve hot with brown rice.

Nutrition Information

Calories
290
Protein
28g
Sodium
95mg
Potassium
720mg

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe has been specially adapted to be DASH-compliant by:

  • Using skinless chicken breast as a lean protein source
  • Incorporating brown rice instead of white rice for added fiber and nutrients
  • Adding bok choy for increased vegetables and minerals
  • Using no added salt, relying on aromatics for flavor
  • Steaming instead of frying to reduce fat content
  • Using sesame oil in moderation for healthy fats
