মসুর ডাল Masoor Dal

A protein-rich Bengali red lentil dish with spinach and aromatic spices. This DASH-friendly version reduces sodium while maintaining authentic flavors.

Servings
4 people
Prep Time
10 mins
Cook Time
20 mins

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Red Lentils 1 cup masoor dal
  • Spinach 2 cups, chopped
  • Tomato 1 medium, chopped

Aromatics

  • Onion 1 small, finely chopped
  • Garlic 3 cloves, minced
  • Ginger 1-inch piece, minced
  • Green Chili 1, finely chopped (optional)

Spices

  • Turmeric 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cumin Seeds 1 teaspoon
  • Olive Oil 2 teaspoons

Garnish

  • Cilantro 2 tablespoons, chopped
  • Lemon 1, cut into wedges

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Wash lentils thoroughly until water runs clear.

  2. 2.

    Heat oil in a pot. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.

  3. 3.

    Add onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until onions are translucent.

  4. 4.

    Add turmeric and green chili. Cook for 30 seconds.

  5. 5.

    Add lentils and 3 cups of water. Bring to a boil.

  6. 6.

    Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.

  7. 7.

    Add tomato and spinach. Cook until spinach wilts.

  8. 8.

    Garnish with cilantro and serve with lemon wedges.

Nutrition Information

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe has been adapted to be DASH-compliant by:

  • Using olive oil instead of traditional cooking fats
  • Adding spinach for extra nutrients and potassium
  • Eliminating added salt and using spices for flavor
  • Including aromatic ingredients for natural flavor enhancement
  • Using lentils as a heart-healthy protein source
