লাবড়া Labra

A nutritious Bengali mixed vegetable curry featuring five different vegetables. This DASH-friendly version is rich in nutrients and low in sodium.

Servings
4 people
Prep Time
20 mins
Cook Time
15 mins

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • Sweet Potato 1 medium, cubed
  • Pumpkin 1 cup, cubed
  • Eggplant 1 small, cubed
  • Green Beans 1 cup, cut
  • Spinach 2 cups, chopped

Aromatics

  • Ginger 1-inch piece, minced
  • Green Chilies 2, slit

Spices

  • Panch Phoron 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cumin Powder 1/2 teaspoon
  • Olive Oil 2 tablespoons

Garnish

  • Cilantro 2 tablespoons, chopped

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Prepare all vegetables: cube sweet potato, pumpkin, and eggplant; cut green beans.

  2. 2.

    Heat oil in a large pan. Add panch phoron and let it splutter.

  3. 3.

    Add ginger and green chilies. Sauté for 30 seconds.

  4. 4.

    Add sweet potato and pumpkin. Cook for 5 minutes.

  5. 5.

    Add eggplant, green beans, turmeric, and cumin powder.

  6. 6.

    Add 1/2 cup water, cover, and cook until vegetables are tender.

  7. 7.

    Add spinach and cook until wilted.

  8. 8.

    Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.

Nutrition Information

Calories
140

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe has been adapted to be DASH-compliant by:

  • Using olive oil instead of traditional cooking fats
  • Including five different vegetables for varied nutrients
  • Adding spinach for extra potassium and minerals
  • Eliminating added salt and using spices for flavor
  • Using sweet potato and pumpkin for natural sweetness
