মাছের ঝোল Machher Jhol

A light and aromatic Bengali fish curry with vegetables and turmeric. This DASH-friendly version reduces sodium while maintaining the traditional flavors.

Servings
4 people
Prep Time
15 mins
Cook Time
20 mins

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Fish 1 pound cod or sea bass fillets
  • Potatoes 2 medium, cubed
  • Cauliflower 1 cup florets
  • Green Peas 1/2 cup

Aromatics

  • Onion 1 medium, sliced
  • Garlic 4 cloves, minced
  • Ginger 1-inch piece, minced
  • Green Chilies 2, slit lengthwise

Spices

  • Turmeric 1 teaspoon
  • Cumin Seeds 1 teaspoon
  • Bay Leaves 2
  • Olive Oil 2 tablespoons

Garnish

  • Cilantro 1/4 cup, chopped

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Rub fish fillets with 1/2 teaspoon turmeric and set aside.

  2. 2.

    Heat oil in a large pan. Add cumin seeds and bay leaves.

  3. 3.

    Add onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until onions are soft.

  4. 4.

    Add remaining turmeric and green chilies. Cook for 1 minute.

  5. 5.

    Add potatoes and cauliflower. Cook for 5 minutes.

  6. 6.

    Add 2 cups water and simmer until vegetables are tender.

  7. 7.

    Gently add fish pieces and green peas. Cook for 5-7 minutes.

  8. 8.

    Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe has been adapted to be DASH-compliant by:

  • Using lean white fish rich in protein and low in saturated fat
  • Adding extra vegetables for increased nutrients and fiber
  • Using olive oil instead of traditional mustard oil
  • Eliminating added salt and using spices for flavor
  • Including potassium-rich ingredients like potatoes and peas
