শুক্তো Shukto

A traditional Bengali mixed vegetable curry featuring bitter gourd and plantains. This DASH-friendly version maintains the dish's unique flavors while promoting heart health.

Servings
4 people
Prep Time
25 mins
Cook Time
20 mins

Ingredients

Vegetables

  • Bitter Gourd 1 medium, sliced
  • Green Plantain 1 medium, sliced
  • Sweet Potato 1 medium, cubed
  • Eggplant 1 small, cubed
  • Drumsticks 2, cut into 4-inch pieces
  • Green Beans 1 cup, cut

Aromatics & Paste

  • Ginger 2-inch piece, made into paste
  • Mustard Seeds 1 teaspoon, ground
  • Poppy Seeds 1 tablespoon, ground

Spices

  • Bay Leaves 2
  • Panch Phoron 1 teaspoon
  • Turmeric 1/2 teaspoon
  • Olive Oil 2 tablespoons

For Serving

  • Milk 1/4 cup, low-fat

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Soak and grind poppy seeds and mustard seeds into a fine paste.

  2. 2.

    Prepare all vegetables: slice bitter gourd and plantain, cube sweet potato and eggplant.

  3. 3.

    Heat oil in a large pan. Add panch phoron and bay leaves.

  4. 4.

    Add bitter gourd and plantain. Cook for 5 minutes.

  5. 5.

    Add remaining vegetables and turmeric. Cook for 5 minutes.

  6. 6.

    Add ground paste, ginger paste, and 1 cup water. Simmer covered.

  7. 7.

    Cook until vegetables are tender but not mushy.

  8. 8.

    Add milk, simmer for 2 minutes. Serve hot.

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe has been adapted to be DASH-compliant by:

  • Using olive oil instead of traditional mustard oil
  • Including a variety of vegetables for increased nutrients
  • Using low-fat milk instead of full-fat milk
  • Eliminating added salt and relying on spices for flavor
  • Maintaining bitter gourd for its natural health benefits
