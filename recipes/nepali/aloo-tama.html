आलु तामा Aloo Tama

A traditional Nepali curry featuring bamboo shoots and potatoes, enriched with black-eyed peas. This DASH-friendly version reduces sodium while preserving authentic flavors.

Servings
4 people
Prep Time
15 mins
Cook Time
25 mins

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Bamboo Shoots 2 cups, sliced
  • Potatoes 2 medium, cubed
  • Black-eyed Peas 1 cup, cooked
  • Tomatoes 2 medium, chopped

Aromatics

  • Onion 1 large, finely chopped
  • Garlic 4 cloves, minced
  • Ginger 1-inch piece, minced

Spices

  • Turmeric 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cumin Seeds 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander 1 teaspoon, ground
  • Olive Oil 2 tablespoons

Garnish

  • Cilantro 1/4 cup, chopped

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Heat oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.

  2. 2.

    Add onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until onions are golden brown.

  3. 3.

    Add turmeric and coriander. Cook for 1 minute.

  4. 4.

    Add tomatoes and cook until they start to break down.

  5. 5.

    Add bamboo shoots and potatoes. Stir well.

  6. 6.

    Add 2 cups water, bring to a boil, then simmer for 15 minutes.

  7. 7.

    Add black-eyed peas and cook for another 5 minutes.

  8. 8.

    Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.

Nutrition Information

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe has been adapted to be DASH-compliant by:

  • Adding black-eyed peas for extra protein and fiber
  • Using olive oil instead of traditional cooking fats
  • Eliminating added salt and using spices for flavor
  • Including potassium-rich vegetables
  • Adding extra tomatoes for natural flavor and nutrients
  • Using fresh herbs and aromatics for taste enhancement
