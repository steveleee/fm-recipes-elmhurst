साग Saag

A nutritious Nepali spiced spinach dish enriched with other leafy greens. This DASH-friendly version is packed with nutrients and flavor.

Servings
4 people
Prep Time
15 mins
Cook Time
15 mins

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Spinach 2 lbs, chopped
  • Mustard Greens 1/2 lb, chopped
  • Fenugreek Leaves 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Green Chilies 2 small, finely chopped

Aromatics

  • Onion 1 medium, finely chopped
  • Garlic 4 cloves, minced
  • Ginger 1-inch piece, minced

Spices

  • Turmeric 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cumin Seeds 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander 1 teaspoon, ground
  • Olive Oil 2 tablespoons

Garnish

  • Lemon 1, cut into wedges

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Wash all greens thoroughly and chop them.

  2. 2.

    Heat oil in a large pot. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.

  3. 3.

    Add onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until onions are translucent.

  4. 4.

    Add turmeric, coriander, and green chilies. Cook for 1 minute.

  5. 5.

    Add all greens and stir well. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.

  6. 6.

    Uncover and cook until greens are tender and excess water evaporates.

  7. 7.

    Serve hot with lemon wedges.

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe has been adapted to be DASH-compliant by:

  • Using a variety of leafy greens for maximum nutrients
  • Using olive oil instead of traditional cooking fats
  • Eliminating added salt and using spices for flavor
  • Including potassium-rich greens
  • Using fresh aromatics for natural flavor enhancement
  • Adding lemon for brightness without sodium
