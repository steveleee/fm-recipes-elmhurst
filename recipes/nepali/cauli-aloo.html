काउली आलु Cauli Aloo

A comforting Nepali curry featuring cauliflower and potatoes, enriched with green peas. This DASH-friendly version maintains authentic flavors while being heart-healthy.

Servings
4 people
Prep Time
15 mins
Cook Time
20 mins

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Cauliflower 1 medium head, cut into florets
  • Potatoes 2 medium, cubed
  • Green Peas 1 cup fresh or frozen
  • Tomatoes 2 medium, chopped

Aromatics

  • Onion 1 large, finely chopped
  • Garlic 4 cloves, minced
  • Ginger 1-inch piece, minced

Spices

  • Turmeric 1/2 teaspoon
  • Cumin Seeds 1 teaspoon
  • Coriander 1 teaspoon, ground
  • Olive Oil 2 tablespoons

Garnish

  • Cilantro 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Lemon 1, cut into wedges

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Heat oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add cumin seeds and let them sizzle.

  2. 2.

    Add onion, garlic, and ginger. Sauté until onions are golden brown.

  3. 3.

    Add turmeric and coriander powder. Cook for 1 minute.

  4. 4.

    Add tomatoes and cook until they soften.

  5. 5.

    Add potatoes and 1/2 cup water. Cover and cook for 5 minutes.

  6. 6.

    Add cauliflower and green peas. Cook for 10-12 minutes until tender.

  7. 7.

    Adjust consistency with water if needed.

  8. 8.

    Garnish with cilantro and serve hot with lemon wedges.

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe has been adapted to be DASH-compliant by:

  • Using olive oil instead of traditional cooking fats
  • Adding green peas for extra fiber and nutrients
  • Eliminating added salt and using spices for flavor
  • Including potassium-rich vegetables
  • Using fresh aromatics for natural flavor enhancement
  • Adding lemon for brightness without sodium
