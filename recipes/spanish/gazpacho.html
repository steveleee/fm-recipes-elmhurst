Gazpacho (Cold Tomato Soup)

A refreshing and healthy Spanish cold soup packed with fresh vegetables. This DASH-friendly version is naturally low in sodium while being rich in vitamins and minerals.

Servings
6 people
Prep Time
20 mins
Chill Time
2 hours

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Tomatoes 2 lbs ripe, chopped
  • Cucumber 1 large, peeled and chopped
  • Bell Pepper 1 red, chopped
  • Onion 1/2 sweet onion, chopped
  • Garlic 2 cloves, minced

Seasonings

  • Olive Oil 2 tablespoons extra virgin
  • Sherry Vinegar 2 tablespoons
  • White Pepper To taste
  • Cumin 1/4 teaspoon ground

Garnish

  • Cucumber 1/2 cup, finely diced
  • Bell Pepper 1/2 cup, finely diced
  • Cherry Tomatoes 1 cup, quartered
  • Herbs Fresh basil or parsley

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Reserve some of each vegetable for garnish, finely diced.

  2. 2.

    Roughly chop remaining vegetables into chunks.

  3. 3.

    Working in batches, blend tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, onion, and garlic until smooth.

  4. 4.

    Add olive oil, vinegar, white pepper, and cumin. Blend until well combined.

  5. 5.

    Strain through a fine-mesh sieve if desired for smoother texture.

  6. 6.

    Chill for at least 2 hours or overnight.

  7. 7.

    Before serving, adjust seasoning to taste.

  8. 8.

    Serve cold, garnished with diced vegetables and fresh herbs.

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe is naturally DASH-compliant and has been optimized by:

  • Using fresh, raw vegetables rich in nutrients and fiber
  • Adding no salt, relying on natural vegetable flavors
  • Including potassium-rich ingredients like tomatoes and cucumbers
  • Using heart-healthy extra virgin olive oil
  • Adding herbs and spices for flavor enhancement
  • Keeping portions moderate while maximizing vegetable content
