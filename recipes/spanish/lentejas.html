Lentejas (Spanish Lentil Stew)

A hearty and nutritious Spanish lentil stew packed with vegetables and aromatic spices. This DASH-friendly version is rich in fiber and protein while being low in sodium.

Servings
6 people
Prep Time
20 mins
Cook Time
30 mins

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • Green Lentils 2 cups, rinsed
  • Carrots 3 large, diced
  • Potatoes 2 medium, cubed
  • Swiss Chard 1 bunch, chopped

Aromatics & Spices

  • Onion 1 large, diced
  • Garlic 6 cloves, minced
  • Bay Leaves 2 leaves
  • Paprika 1 tablespoon
  • Cumin 1 teaspoon
  • Thyme 2 sprigs, fresh

Additional

  • Olive Oil 2 tablespoons
  • Tomatoes 2 large, diced
  • Stock 6 cups low-sodium vegetable stock
  • Apple Cider Vinegar 1 tablespoon

Garnish

  • Parsley 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Lemon 1, cut into wedges

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.

  2. 2.

    Saut√© onions until softened, about 5 minutes.

  3. 3.

    Add garlic, paprika, and cumin. Cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.

  4. 4.

    Add lentils, carrots, potatoes, tomatoes, bay leaves, and thyme.

  5. 5.

    Pour in vegetable stock and bring to a boil.

  6. 6.

    Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 20-25 minutes until lentils are tender.

  7. 7.

    Add Swiss chard and cook for additional 5 minutes.

  8. 8.

    Stir in apple cider vinegar.

  9. 9.

    Remove bay leaves and thyme sprigs.

  10. 10.

    Serve hot, garnished with parsley and lemon wedges.

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe has been adapted to be DASH-compliant by:

  • Using lentils as a plant-based protein source rich in fiber
  • Including a variety of vegetables for increased nutrients
  • Using low-sodium vegetable stock
  • Adding Swiss chard for extra minerals and vitamins
  • Using herbs and spices for flavor instead of salt
  • Including heart-healthy olive oil in moderation
