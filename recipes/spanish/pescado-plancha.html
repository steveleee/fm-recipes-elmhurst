Pescado a la Plancha (Grilled Fish)

A light and flavorful Spanish grilled fish dish with Mediterranean vegetables. This DASH-friendly version uses herbs and citrus for flavor while keeping sodium low.

Servings
4 people
Prep Time
15 mins
Cook Time
15 mins

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • White Fish 4 fillets (sea bass or cod), about 6 oz each
  • Zucchini 2 medium, sliced
  • Bell Peppers 2 mixed colors, sliced
  • Cherry Tomatoes 2 cups, halved
  • Asparagus 1 bunch, trimmed

Marinade & Seasonings

  • Olive Oil 3 tablespoons
  • Garlic 4 cloves, minced
  • Lemon 2, one juiced, one for serving
  • Oregano 1 tablespoon, fresh
  • Thyme 2 sprigs, fresh
  • White Pepper To taste

Garnish

  • Parsley 1/4 cup, chopped
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil For drizzling

Instructions

  1. 1.

    Mix olive oil, garlic, lemon juice, oregano, and white pepper for the marinade.

  2. 2.

    Brush fish fillets with marinade and let rest for 10 minutes.

  3. 3.

    Toss vegetables with remaining marinade.

  4. 4.

    Heat a grill pan or plancha over medium-high heat.

  5. 5.

    Grill vegetables until tender-crisp, about 6-8 minutes. Set aside and keep warm.

  6. 6.

    Grill fish for 3-4 minutes per side until cooked through.

  7. 7.

    Arrange vegetables on plates, top with fish.

  8. 8.

    Garnish with parsley, drizzle with extra virgin olive oil, and serve with lemon wedges.

Nutrition Information

Calories
290
Protein

Sodium

Potassium
890mg

DASH Diet Notes

This recipe has been adapted to be DASH-compliant by:

  • Using lean white fish as a healthy protein source
  • Including a variety of colorful vegetables for nutrients
  • Using herbs, garlic, and lemon for flavor instead of salt
  • Incorporating heart-healthy olive oil
  • Adding potassium-rich vegetables like bell peppers and asparagus
  • Using a healthy cooking method (grilling) that requires minimal added fats
