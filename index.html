DASH Diet Recipe Guide

The DASH diet (Dietary Approaches to Stop Hypertension) is a proven approach to reduce blood pressure and improve heart health. It emphasizes:

  • Vegetables, fruits, and whole grains
  • Lean proteins and heart-healthy fats
  • Limited sodium and processed foods
  • Rich in potassium, magnesium, and calcium

These recipes have been carefully adapted to follow DASH principles while preserving authentic flavors and ingredients.
